Joining the Agora

Please review this Agora's base contract to verify you are in agreement, then send email to signup@anagora.org with the following information:
  • The URL of a git repository containing your digital garden, wiki or blog; or a link to the content you would like to contribute in any format.
  • Your desired username (you can check for existing users here).
Please also reach out if you are interested in joining but do not have a git repository currently or otherwise need assistance for importing your contributions into the Agora.

We are working on making this process less manual and more user friendly and inclusive :) Thank you for your patience!

Contributing from social media

Alternatively, you can contribute in a limited way from the Fediverse by following and interacting with @agora@botsin.space; or from Bluesky by following and interacting with anagora.bsky.social.
